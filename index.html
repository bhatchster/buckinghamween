About

The Band

Buckingham Ween is Southern California's premier tribute to Ween — a band formed when the Boognish spoke to Gene and Dean. We bring you the full spectrum of the Ween catalog, from lo-fi early chaos and country twang, through the Grobe and nautical psychedelia, which all end up making Brown.

Built by Ween devotees, Buckingham Ween delivers the kind of live experience that will hold you over until the Boognish rises again.

"Was such a great show. It was great seeing some Ween songs live. You guys were fantastic. We came down from Bakersfield, can't wait to see you guys again!"
